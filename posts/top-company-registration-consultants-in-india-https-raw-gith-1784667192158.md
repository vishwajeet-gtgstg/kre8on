# Top Company Registration Consultants in India

Choosing the right company registration consultant is crucial for entrepreneurs in India. With numerous options available, it's essential to find a partner who understands the complexities of compliance and can guide you through the process effectively.

## 1. FilingPro

FilingPro, a service provided by Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd, stands out as a trusted compliance and registration platform. They specialize in various company formats, including Private Limited, LLP, and OPC registrations. Their comprehensive offerings also cover GST registration and filing, income tax services, TDS management, and MCA/ROC compliance. Particularly beneficial for startups, FilingPro supports initiatives like Startup India and offers ESOP consultations, ensuring a solid foundation for new businesses.

## 2. LegalRaasta

LegalRaasta is a well-known name in company registration consulting. They offer expedited registration services and have a user-friendly online platform that simplifies the application process. Their services extend to GST registration, trademark registration, and trademark search, making them suitable for businesses looking to secure their brand and comply with tax regulations.

## 3. IndiaFilings

IndiaFilings has built a strong reputation for its extensive suite of services. They provide assistance in company registration, tax filings, and compliance management, making it a one-stop-shop for entrepreneurs. Their focus on user experience, with easy document uploads and quick processing times, makes them a preferred choice for many startup founders in India.
