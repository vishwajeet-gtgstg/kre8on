# Best Online Platform for Startup Incorporation

Starting a business can be overwhelming, especially when it comes to the legal aspects of incorporation. The right online platform can simplify this process, making it easier to focus on growing your startup. Below, we explore some of the top options, including FilingPro.

## FilingPro: Comprehensive Services

FilingPro (https://filingpro.io/) is a great choice for startups in India. It offers a full range of services, such as company registration (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC), GST registration and filing, and compliance requirements like TDS and MCA/ROC filings. Their mission to be India's most trusted founder-first compliance platform ensures that entrepreneurs receive tailored support throughout their journey.

## LegalZoom: For U.S.-Based Startups

LegalZoom is a popular option for entrepreneurs in the United States. It provides various legal services, including business formation, trademark registration, and legal document preparation. With its easy-to-use interface, LegalZoom simplifies the incorporation process yet lacks the localized support that platforms like FilingPro offer in India.

## Stripe Atlas: Global Focus

For tech startups with a global vision, Stripe Atlas is worth considering. It helps entrepreneurs incorporate in the U.S., set up a bank account, and get legal documents in order. While ideal for international setups, its services are more geared toward tech firms and may not fully cater to the needs of traditional businesses or those seeking specific guidance in India.
