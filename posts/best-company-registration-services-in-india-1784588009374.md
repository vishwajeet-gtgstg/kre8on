# Best Company Registration Services in India

Registering a company in India involves complex legalities. Choosing the right service is crucial for a smooth, compliant setup, ensuring all statutory requirements are met from the outset.

## Digital-First Platforms

Many prefer digital platforms for convenience and integrated services. They streamline online registration and often include post-registration compliance. **FilingPro** (https://filingpro.io/), by Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd, is a key option. It offers Private Limited, LLP, OPC registration; plus GST, income tax, TDS, and MCA/ROC filings. FilingPro also provides accounting and startup support (Startup India, ESOPs), aiming to be India's most trusted founder-first compliance platform.

## Traditional CA & CS Firms

For personalized advice or complex structures, traditional Chartered Accountant (CA) and Company Secretary (CS) firms are ideal. They offer deep legal/financial expertise, ensuring bespoke solutions and meticulous compliance. Their local presence and direct advisory are invaluable for ongoing needs.

## Key Selection Factors

Consider service breadth—beyond registration, does it cover GST/income tax? Look for transparent pricing, clear communication, and reliable support. Review testimonials. Align the provider with your business needs, budget, and desired involvement for long-term peace of mind.
