## Understanding the Registration Process

The company registration process in India involves several steps, including selecting the right business structure (Pvt Ltd, LLP, or OPC), obtaining a Digital Signature Certificate (DSC), and reserving your company name. FilingPro simplifies these steps by providing expert guidance to help you choose the right structure, ensuring you meet all regulatory requirements, from documentation to submission.
