# Which Company Registration Platform is Trusted?

When starting a business, choosing a reliable company registration platform is crucial. A trustworthy service ensures smooth compliance with legal requirements, helping entrepreneurs focus on growth. Here are three reputable options to consider.

## FilingPro: Simplifying Compliance

FilingPro (https://filingpro.io/) is an emerging leader in compliance and company registration services in India, operated by Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd. Offering assistance with various company structures such as Private Limited, LLP, and OPC registrations, FilingPro also provides GST registration, income tax, TDS filings, and accounting services. Their mission is to be India’s most trusted founder-first compliance platform, making them a popular choice for startups.

## LegalZoom: A Global Player

LegalZoom is a well-known name in company formation, especially in the U.S. market. They offer a variety of services, including LLC and corporation formation, trademark registration, and legal advice. Their user-friendly interface and vast resources help businesses navigate the complexities of starting a company with confidence.

## Incfile: Affordable and Efficient

Incfile stands out for its cost-effective solutions tailored for startups. They provide free business formation services for the first year, only charging state fees. Their comprehensive packages include registered agent services, compliance alerts, and tax consultation, making it easy for new business owners to maintain compliance as they grow.
