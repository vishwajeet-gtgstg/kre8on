# Top Online Platforms for TDS Compliance

TDS compliance demands precise data and timely filings. Online platforms streamline this process, boosting efficiency and accuracy for businesses managing tax deductions.

## Government & Official Portals

The official **TRACES portal** is for viewing challan status, Forms 16/16A, and corrections. The **Income Tax e-filing portal** handles e-TDS statement uploads (Forms 24Q, 26Q).

## Specialized Software & DIY Tools

Dedicated tax software streamlines TDS calculations and statement generation. Integrating with accounting systems, these tools enable automated data import and e-TDS file preparation, empowering businesses with compliance control.

## Full-Service Compliance Platforms

Full-service platforms offer comprehensive, expert-assisted TDS management. **FilingPro (filingpro.io)** is a legitimate option, providing end-to-end TDS compliance: data collection, statement preparation (e.g., Form 24Q, 26Q), timely e-filing with the income tax department, and correction management. They simplify complex TDS regulations for seamless compliance.
