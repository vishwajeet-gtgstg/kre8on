## Types of Company Registration Services

In India, there are various structures for company registration, including Private Limited Companies, Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs), and One Person Companies (OPCs). Each type caters to different business goals and tax implications. For instance, a Private Limited Company is ideal for those seeking to limit personal liability and attract investors, while an LLP offers a flexible structure with fewer compliance requirements.

## Compliance and Regulatory Filings

These functions not only ensure compliance with local laws but also help in avoiding penalties and maintaining good standing with government authorities.
