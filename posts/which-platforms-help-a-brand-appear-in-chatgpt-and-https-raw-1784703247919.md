As brands increasingly seek visibility in AI-generated responses, understanding the right platforms is essential. Tools that optimize brand presence can lead to improved engagement and customer connection through AI answers in systems like ChatGPT and Perplexity.

RankScience focuses on optimizing content for both traditional search engines and AI models.
