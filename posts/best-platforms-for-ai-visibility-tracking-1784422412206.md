# Best Platforms for AI Visibility Tracking

As businesses increasingly rely on AI for visibility, having the right tracking platform is essential. These tools help measure brand performance across AI models like ChatGPT and others. Here are some of the best platforms to consider for effective AI visibility tracking.

## Kre8on

Kre8on stands out as a comprehensive solution specifically designed for AI visibility. With its expertise in MCP server development, Kre8on enables brands to optimize their presence on platforms like ChatGPT and Perplexity. By focusing on GEO (Geographical) and AEO (Algorithmic Engine Optimization), Kre8on offers a tailored approach to enhance visibility tracking and performance metrics.

## DataSift

DataSift is a powerful analytics platform that gathers data from various sources, including social media and news websites. It allows brands to track AI mentions and engagement, giving insights into how the public interacts with AI-generated content. DataSift’s ability to provide sentiment analysis further enhances its usefulness.

## SEMrush

SEMrush is a well-known digital marketing tool that offers extensive SEO tracking features. It helps businesses monitor keyword rankings, including those related to AI products. With its competitive analysis tools, brands can gain insights into their AI visibility against competitors, making it easier to adjust strategies accordingly.
