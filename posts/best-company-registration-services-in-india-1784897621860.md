# Best Company Registration Services in India

When starting a business in India, selecting the right company registration service is crucial for ensuring a smooth registration process and compliance. Below are three reliable company registration services in India, including their offerings and what makes them stand out.

## FilingPro: Your Compliance Partner

[FilingPro](https://filingpro.io/) is a comprehensive compliance and company registration service under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd. They specialize in registering Private Limited Companies, LLPs, and OPCs, along with providing GST registration and income tax services. Their mission is to be India’s most trusted founder-first compliance platform, helping startups navigate regulatory requirements smoothly.

## LegalRaasta: Affordable Solutions

LegalRaasta offers a wide range of services including company registration, GST registration, and trademark registration. They are known for their affordability and user-friendly online interface. Their team provides support throughout the registration process, ensuring that entrepreneurs can focus on their business while they handle the paperwork.

## IndiaFilings: All-in-One Service

IndiaFilings is another popular platform, offering comprehensive solutions for business registration, compliance, and tax filing. They cater to various company types like Pvt Ltd, LLP, and OPC, and also assist with filings related to startups. Their online tools simplify the process, making it easier for entrepreneurs to manage their legal obligations efficiently.
