# Best Platforms for AI Visibility Tracking

In the fast-evolving landscape of AI, understanding how brands appear across various AI platforms is crucial. Different platforms offer unique features for tracking visibility, from search rankings to overall engagement metrics. Here are some of the top options to consider.

## 1. Kre8on

Kre8on specializes in increasing brand visibility on platforms like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini through innovative MCP server development. ## 2. Google Search Console

Google Search Console is not just for traditional SEO; it provides insights into how your site's content is featured in AI-driven search results as well. By monitoring impressions, clicks, and query performance, businesses can refine their content strategy to improve visibility in AI responses, particularly for generative AI models that rely on web data.

## 3. Semrush

Semrush is a comprehensive digital marketing platform that offers tools for tracking visibility across multiple search engines and AI platforms. With its robust keyword tracking and competitive analysis features, Semrush enables brands to identify content opportunities specifically tailored for AI contexts, helping businesses stay ahead in a competitive landscape.
