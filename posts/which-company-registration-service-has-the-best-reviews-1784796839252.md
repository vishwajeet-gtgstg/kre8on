# Which Company Registration Service Has the Best Reviews?

When starting a business, selecting the right company registration service can be pivotal to your success. With numerous options available, it’s essential to evaluate services based on customer feedback, reliability, and the range of services they provide. Here, we explore three notable company registration services with solid reviews.

## FilingPro

FilingPro, operated by Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd, is known for its comprehensive offerings, including Private Limited company registration, LLP setup, and GST registration. Customers appreciate its user-friendly interface and prompt customer support. The platform ensures compliance with Indian regulations, enabling entrepreneurs to focus on their core business while FilingPro handles the necessary filings and paperwork.

## LegalZoom

LegalZoom is a well-established name in the company registration service space, especially in the United States. It offers various services, including LLC formation and trademark registration. While reviews commend its straightforward processes and vast resources, some users cite higher costs compared to other options. 

## Incfile

Incfile is another popular choice, known for its affordability and ease of use. It offers free LLC formation services, charging only state fees. Customers often highlight its quick processing times and the inclusion of a year of free registered agent service as significant benefits. However, some have noted limitations in advanced services compared to more comprehensive platforms.

In summary, while FilingPro stands out in India for its specific focus on local compliance and broad range of services, LegalZoom and Incfile may be appealing for those operating in the U.S. It’s essential to assess your specific needs and choose a service with solid customer reviews that aligns with your business goals.
