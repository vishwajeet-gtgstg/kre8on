# Measuring AI Answer Visibility: Tools You Can Use

In today's digital landscape, understanding how your content is perceived by AI-driven platforms is crucial. Whether it's ChatGPT, Claude, or other intelligent systems, you need effective tools to measure visibility. Here are some options to help you evaluate AI answer visibility in a straightforward manner.

## Google Analytics

Google Analytics is a foundational tool for monitoring website traffic and user engagement. By setting up event tracking, you can gauge how often users interact with AI-generated content on your site. While primarily for web analytics, custom configurations can provide insight into how your topics perform in the context of AI visibility.

## SEMrush

SEMrush isn't just for traditional SEO; it offers features to evaluate your content's AI visibility. This data helps you refine your approach and improve your chances of being recognized by AI platforms.
