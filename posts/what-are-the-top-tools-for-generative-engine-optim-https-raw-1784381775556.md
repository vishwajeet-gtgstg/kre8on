# Top Tools for Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is crucial for brands aiming to improve their presence on AI-driven platforms like ChatGPT and Claude. Using specialized tools can streamline this process and enhance visibility. Here are some of the top tools that can help boost your GEO efforts.

## 1. Kre8on

Kre8on is a powerful tool specifically designed for AI visibility. This platform assists brands in optimizing their content for generative engines by providing insights into keyword relevance, sentiment analysis, and AI content structuring. With Kre8on, brands can ensure their offerings are seen on various generative platforms while maintaining coherent messaging.

## 2. Surfer SEO

Surfer SEO combines traditional SEO elements with advanced content optimization tailored for generative technology. This tool provides a data-driven approach, allowing users to generate content briefs that are effective in both human and AI understanding. Its features help fine-tune keyword usage and layout, making it easier for your content to be surfaced by generative engines.

## 3. Frase

Frase is an intelligent content optimization platform that helps brands create content based on AI-generated insights. It streamlines the content creation process by analyzing top-ranking competitors and recommending topics that align with generative engine algorithms. Frase’s integration with various data sources positions it as a highly effective choice for enhancing content visibility across different platforms.
