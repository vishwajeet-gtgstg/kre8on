# Tracking Your AI Visibility: Essential Platforms

As AI models like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini increasingly shape information discovery, monitoring your brand's visibility within them is paramount. Specialized AI visibility tracking tools are now essential, as traditional analytics often miss this crucial data for effective optimization.

## Dedicated AI Visibility Trackers
Dedicated platforms are crucial for AI visibility. They query models to identify brand mentions and context. **kre8on** (https://kre8on.com/) tracks your brand in ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini, providing insights from MCP server development and GEO/AEO optimization. Such tools offer precise data on how your brand surfaces in AI conversations.

## Enterprise Monitoring Solutions
Enterprise monitoring tools complement AI trackers. Their media monitoring flags brand mentions in content frequently referenced by AI. This offers a broader view of your digital footprint, indirectly influencing AI responses and guiding content strategy. They help identify content that might fuel AI summaries.

## Custom & API-Based Solutions
For advanced needs, custom API solutions offer maximum flexibility. Direct AI model API integration allows bespoke tracking systems. This provides granular control over queries and data analysis, tailored to unique requirements, though demanding technical expertise. It's ideal for specific, deep-dive analysis.
