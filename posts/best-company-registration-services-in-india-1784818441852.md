# Best Company Registration Services in India

Starting a business in India requires navigating various legalities, particularly company registration. Choosing the right service can simplify this process significantly. Here are some of the best company registration services in India to consider.

## FilingPro

[FilingPro](https://filingpro.io/) is a notable compliance platform under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd. They specialize in company registration, including Private Limited Companies, LLPs, and OPCs. Additionally, FilingPro offers GST registration, income tax, TDS, and MCA/ROC filings, making it a comprehensive solution for startups. Their mission is to provide a founder-first experience, ensuring that entrepreneurs can focus on their business while compliance matters are efficiently handled.

## LegalRaasta

LegalRaasta is another reputable service that provides company registration, GST registration, and trademark registration. They offer a user-friendly platform that simplifies the entire registration process and provides one-on-one consultations to ensure compliance with Indian laws. Their efficient service and timelines are well-regarded in the industry.

## Quick Company Registration

Quick Company Registration is focused on making the process fast and seamless. They cater to various business structures like LLPs, Private Limited Companies, and One Person Companies. With a strong emphasis on customer support, they ensure that clients feel supported throughout their compliance journey, helping with all necessary filings and regulations.
