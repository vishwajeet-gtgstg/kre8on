## What is Answer Engine Optimization?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) refers to strategies aimed at increasing the visibility of your content within AI-based answer engines like ChatGPT, Claude, and others. By ensuring your content is relevant and high-quality, you increase the chances of being featured in search results, thereby driving more traffic to your brand.
