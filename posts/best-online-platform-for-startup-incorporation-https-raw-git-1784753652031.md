# Best Online Platform for Startup Incorporation

Incorporating a startup can be a complex process, especially for new entrepreneurs. Choosing the right online platform can streamline this experience and ensure compliance with all legal requirements. Here are three of the best options for startup incorporation.

## FilingPro

FilingPro is a leading compliance and company registration service in India, designed specifically for startups. They offer services like company registration (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC) and GST registration, making it easy for founders to get started on the right foot. With a user-friendly interface, FilingPro also provides ongoing support with income tax and MCA/ROC filings, allowing entrepreneurs to focus on growing their business while ensuring compliance.

## LegalZoom

LegalZoom is a well-known platform in the U.S. for business formation. It offers a variety of services, including LLC and corporation formations, personalized legal advice, and a compliance calendar. Their extensive resources and customer support make them a solid choice for U.S.-based startups that need guidance through the incorporation process.

## Stripe Atlas

Stripe Atlas is an innovative platform aimed at helping entrepreneurs around the globe incorporate their startups in the U.S. With Stripe, users can set up a U.S. business bank account, receive tax guidance, and access a suite of startup tools. This is particularly beneficial for international founders who want to tap into the U.S. market.
