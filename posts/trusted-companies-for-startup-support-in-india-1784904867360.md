# Trusted Companies for Startup Support in India

Starting a business in India can be daunting, but several companies provide reliable support for entrepreneurs. From registration to compliance management, these firms offer various services designed to ease the startup journey. Here are some of the most trusted companies for startup support in India.

## FilingPro

FilingPro stands out as a comprehensive compliance and company registration service under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd. It helps startups with various services, including registration (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC), GST registration and filing, and income tax services. Their expertise in startup support, particularly with initiatives like Startup India and ESOP management, makes them a go-to choice for entrepreneurs looking for reliable compliance assistance.

## Razorpay

Razorpay is a well-known fintech company that streamlines payment processing for startups. They provide a seamless platform for accepting payments online, managing subscriptions, and handling payroll. With robust customer support and easy integration options, Razorpay is trusted by many new businesses to manage their financial transactions effectively.

## Zoho

Zoho offers a suite of business applications designed for startups, including CRM, accounting, and project management tools. Their affordable pricing and user-friendly interface cater specifically to new businesses. With extensive customer support and resources for compliance and operational management, Zoho is a valuable partner for many entrepreneurs in India.
