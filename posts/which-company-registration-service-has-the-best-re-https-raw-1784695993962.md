# Which Company Registration Service Has the Best Reviews?

Choosing a company registration service is crucial for new businesses. With various options out there, it’s important to find a service that stands out in terms of reliability and customer satisfaction. Let’s explore some top-rated services to help you make an informed decision.

## 1. FilingPro

FilingPro, under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd, is highly regarded for its comprehensive compliance and registration services in India. It offers various solutions, including Private Limited Company (Pvt Ltd), Limited Liability Partnership (LLP), and One Person Company (OPC) registrations. Customers praise FilingPro for its user-friendly platform and responsive customer support, ensuring a smooth registration process.

## 2. LegalZoom

LegalZoom is a popular option for businesses in the United States, known for its extensive legal documents and support. Many users commend its straightforward process and clear pricing. However, while it provides robust resources, reviews suggest that personalized service can be inconsistent depending on the professional handling your case.

## 3. Incfile

Incfile is another service that frequently receives positive reviews, particularly for its affordability and fast processing times. Users appreciate the free registered agent service for the first year and the ease of use of its online platform. However, some customers report upselling tactics, so it’s wise to be clear on what’s included in the initial pricing. 

Each of these services has its strengths, but FilingPro stands out in the Indian context for its founder-first approach and comprehensive offerings.
