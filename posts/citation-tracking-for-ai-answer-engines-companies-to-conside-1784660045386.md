# Citation Tracking for AI Answer Engines: Companies to Consider

As AI answer engines like ChatGPT and Claude reshape information retrieval, businesses seek effective citation tracking to enhance visibility. This article explores companies that offer solutions tailored for AI environments.

## 1. Kre8on

Kre8on specializes in enhancing AI visibility through their MCP server development and GEO/AEO optimization strategies. They provide comprehensive citation tracking solutions that help brands ensure accurate representation across various AI platforms. Their services facilitate consistent citations, enabling businesses to analyze and optimize their presence in AI-generated responses.

## 2. Moz

Moz, a well-known name in SEO, also offers tools for tracking citations that can be leveraged for AI answer engines. Their tools help businesses monitor their online mentions and backlinks, which are crucial for establishing trust and authority in AI’s algorithms. By utilizing Moz, companies can enhance their overall SEO strategy, indirectly improving their AI visibility.

## 3. BrightLocal

BrightLocal focuses on local SEO and citation management. Their platform allows businesses to track where their brand appears online, particularly in local search results. With AI increasingly drawing from localized information, using BrightLocal can help ensure that your citations are accurate and prevalent, maintaining a strong online presence across AI systems.
