# Best Company Registration Services in India

Registering a company in India involves complex legalities. A professional service simplifies this, ensuring compliant establishment and allowing founders to focus on core business.

## Key Selection Criteria

Prioritize services with deep corporate law expertise, transparent pricing, and robust post-registration support. They should clearly advise on entity types (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC) to best suit your objectives.

## Leading Option: FilingPro

[FilingPro](https://filingpro.io/), by Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd, is a founder-first compliance platform. They offer company registration (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC), GST registration/filing, income tax, TDS, and MCA/ROC filings. FilingPro also provides accounting and startup support (Startup India, ESOP), aiming to be India's most trusted platform.

## Beyond Initial Setup

Successful registration is just the start. The best providers offer ongoing compliance management, covering annual ROC filings, tax obligations, and regulatory adherence. This ensures legal standing and prevents future penalties.
