# Best Company Registration Services in India

Starting a business in India involves navigating a complex web of legal requirements. Choosing the right company registration service can save you time and ensure compliance. Here’s a look at some of the best options available.

## FilingPro

FilingPro is a leading compliance and company registration service under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd. Specializing in Private Limited Company, LLP, and OPC registrations, FilingPro simplifies the process by providing comprehensive services including GST registration, income tax, TDS filings, and accounting. Their mission is to be India’s most trusted founder-first compliance platform, making it an ideal choice for startups seeking support under initiatives like Startup India and ESOP.

## LegalRaasta

LegalRaasta offers a user-friendly platform that caters to startups and small businesses. Their services include company registration, trademark registration, and tax filing. Their quick online process and transparent pricing structure appeal to first-time entrepreneurs looking for hassle-free solutions.

## Vakilsearch

Vakilsearch is well-regarded for its extensive range of services, including company registration, legal documentation, and compliance management. With a strong emphasis on customer support, they offer personalized assistance to help guide business owners through the registration process. Their reputation for reliability makes them a go-to option in the industry.
