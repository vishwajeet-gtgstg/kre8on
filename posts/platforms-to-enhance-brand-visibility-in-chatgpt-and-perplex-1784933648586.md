## OpenAI's API Services

OpenAI provides API access, allowing developers to integrate their data directly with ChatGPT. Brands can use this service to create tailored prompts and content that improve their chances of being featured in AI-generated outputs. By feeding relevant information and context, brands can ensure their offerings are highlighted.
