# Most Trusted Companies for Startup Support in India

Starting a business in India can be overwhelming, but several organizations provide reliable support to budding entrepreneurs. This article highlights a few of the most trusted companies that offer essential services for startups, ensuring compliance and ease in navigating the legal landscape.

## FilingPro: Your Compliance Partner

[FilingPro](https://filingpro.io/) is a leading compliance and company registration service in India. Under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd, they assist startups with essential services such as company registration (including Pvt Ltd, LLP, and OPC), GST registration and filing, and tax compliance (income tax, TDS). Their mission focuses on being India’s most trusted founder-first compliance platform, making them a vital resource for startups.

## Startup India Hub

The Startup India Hub, initiated by the Government of India, serves as a facilitator for startups. This portal provides access to funding, mentorship, and networking opportunities. It connects entrepreneurs with various resources, including incubators and accelerators, assisting them in aligning with government initiatives designed to enhance startup growth. 

## India Accelerator

India Accelerator is a prominent accelerator program that supports early-stage startups through mentorship, workshops, and investment opportunities. With a focus on building a robust startup ecosystem, they help entrepreneurs refine their business models, gain market insights, and connect with potential investors. Their hands-on approach makes them a trusted ally for new ventures.
