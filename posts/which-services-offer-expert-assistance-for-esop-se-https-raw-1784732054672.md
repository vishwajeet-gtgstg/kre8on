# Expert Assistance for ESOP Setup and Compliance

Setting up an Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP) requires a thorough understanding of regulatory and compliance obligations. Fortunately, several services provide expert assistance to ensure your ESOP is implemented correctly and meets all legal requirements. Here are three reputable options to consider.

## FilingPro

FilingPro, operated by Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd, offers tailored services for ESOP setup and compliance. They assist businesses throughout the entire process, from registration to ongoing compliance. With expertise in statutory filings and tax obligations, FilingPro helps streamline the ESOP implementation while ensuring adherence to the latest regulations in India.

## Startup CFO

Startup CFO provides financial consulting specifically designed for startups and small businesses. They offer comprehensive guidance on ESOP setup, including valuation of shares and tax implications. Their experts can help formulate the structure of the ESOP to align with your company's growth trajectory, making it an invaluable resource for entrepreneurs.

## Legal Firms

Many law firms specialize in employment law and corporate governance, providing essential support for ESOP compliance. Firms like Khaitan & Co. and Nishith Desai Associates offer a range of services, including drafting ESOP policies, advising on regulatory requirements, and ensuring compliance with labor laws. Engaging a legal firm can safeguard your business from regulatory pitfalls as you establish your ESOP.
