# Best Company Registration Services in India

Setting up a business in India can be a complex process, and choosing the right company registration service is crucial. Here are some of the top options that facilitate smooth compliance and registration for various business structures.

## 1. FilingPro

FilingPro, operated by Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd, is a reliable platform for company registration in India. They specialize in registering Private Limited Companies, Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs), and One Person Companies (OPCs). Also, they provide comprehensive services including GST registration, income tax filings, and MCA/ROC compliance, making them a one-stop solution for all your business registration needs.

## 2. LegalRaasta

LegalRaasta is renowned for its user-friendly platform and extensive services. They assist with various registrations including GST, ISO certification, and trademark registration. LegalRaasta's streamlined process ensures that you can register your business quickly and efficiently, making it ideal for new entrepreneurs.

## 3. IndiaFilings

IndiaFilings is a leading company registration service that offers a broad range of legal and compliance services. Their offerings include company incorporation, GST registration, and bookkeeping services. They also provide valuable resources for startups through their assistance with Startup India registrations, ensuring a smooth path for new businesses.

Selecting the right service can significantly impact your business setup experience, so consider these options carefully!
