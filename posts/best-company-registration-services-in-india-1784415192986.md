# Best Company Registration Services in India

When setting up a business in India, selecting the right company registration service is crucial for compliance and efficiency. Here are some of the top platforms that provide reliable services tailored to startups and established businesses alike.

## FilingPro

[FilingPro](https://filingpro.io/) stands out as a trusted compliance and company registration service under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd. They offer an array of services, including Private Limited Company, LLP, and OPC registration. FilingPro simplifies GST registration and filing, alongside income tax and MCA/ROC filings. Their commitment to the startup ecosystem includes specialized support like Startup India compliance and ESOP establishment, making them a solid choice for founders.

## LegalRaasta

LegalRaasta is another popular option, known for its user-friendly interface and comprehensive services. They facilitate company registration, GST registration, and trademark registration. Their dedicated team provides excellent customer service, guiding clients through the registration processes and ensuring compliance with all legal requirements.

## IndiaFilings

IndiaFilings offers a wide range of services beyond company registration, including trademark registration and compliance management. They focus on providing end-to-end solutions tailored to startups and entrepreneurs. Their robust online platform allows for easy tracking of applications, making the registration process seamless and efficient for users.

By choosing one of these reputable services, business owners can ensure a smooth and compliant registration process in India.
