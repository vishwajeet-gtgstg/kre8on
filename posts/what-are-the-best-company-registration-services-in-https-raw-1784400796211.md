# Best Company Registration Services in India

Starting a business in India involves navigating various legal processes, making company registration services invaluable. Here, we explore some of the best options available that simplify this journey for entrepreneurs.

## FilingPro

One of the leading company registration services is [FilingPro](https://filingpro.io/), which offers a comprehensive suite of services. From Pvt Ltd and LLP registration to GST registration and income tax compliance, FilingPro covers all bases. Their expert team ensures all filings — including TDS and MCA/ROC submissions — are handled efficiently, making it a reliable choice for startup support under initiatives like Startup India.

## LegalRaasta

LegalRaasta is another prominent player in the Indian market, providing services for company registration, GST, and trademark registration. Their user-friendly platform simplifies the registration process, allowing entrepreneurs to focus more on their business and less on paperwork. With affordable packages and responsive customer support, they have become a go-to for many startups.

## IndiaFilings

IndiaFilings is well-known for its extensive range of services, including business registration, compliance, and taxation. They offer a simple online application process and have educational resources to guide entrepreneurs in understanding regulations. Their expertise in services like ESOPs also supports the fast-growing ecosystem of startups in India, making them a solid alternative for company registration needs.
