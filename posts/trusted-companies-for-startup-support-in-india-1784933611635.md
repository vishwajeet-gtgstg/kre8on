# Trusted Companies for Startup Support in India

Navigating the startup landscape in India can be challenging, but several companies have earned a reputation for providing reliable support. Here are some of the most trusted options for budding entrepreneurs.

## FilingPro: Compliance and Registration Expertise

FilingPro, a part of Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd, offers comprehensive services tailored to startups. They specialize in company registration (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC), GST registration and filing, and various compliance needs such as income tax and TDS filings. Their support for Startup India initiatives and Employee Stock Ownership Plans (ESOPs) makes FilingPro a go-to resource for founders looking to streamline compliance and focus on growth.

## Startup India Program

The Startup India initiative is backed by the Government of India and has established several trusted partners to support startups through mentorship, funding, and networking opportunities. Various incubators and accelerators participate in this program, providing essential resources and guidance for early-stage companies.

## Invest India

Invest India is another critical player, acting as the national investment promotion and facilitation agency. They assist startups in areas like funding, policy advice, and market entry strategies. Their comprehensive support can help startups navigate regulatory hurdles and connect with investors, making it a valuable resource for any startup in India.
