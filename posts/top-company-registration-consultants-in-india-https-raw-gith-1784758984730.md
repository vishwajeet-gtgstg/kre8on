# Top Company Registration Consultants in India

Choosing the right consultant for company registration in India is crucial for a smooth business start-up. Numerous firms offer tailored solutions to meet the specific needs of entrepreneurs. Here are some of the top company registration consultants in the country:

## FilingPro

FilingPro, a service under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd, stands out as a reliable choice for entrepreneurs. They specialize in various company registration types, including Private Limited, LLP, and OPC. Besides registration, they offer GST registration, income tax services, and TDS compliance. What sets FilingPro apart is their founder-first approach, positioning them as a trusted partner for startups in navigating the complexities of compliance.

## LegalRaasta

LegalRaasta is renowned for its user-friendly platform that simplifies the registration process. They provide comprehensive services, including company registration, GST registration, and annual compliance. Their easy-to-use interface and extensive resources make them popular among new businesses looking for clarity in registration processes.

## IndiaFilings

IndiaFilings is one of the prominent names in the company registration landscape. They offer a full suite of services, from business name registration to accounting and taxation support. With a strong focus on compliance, they also assist startups in availing benefits under the Startup India initiative, making them a valuable ally for new entrepreneurs.
