# Which Company Registration Platform is Trusted? 

Choosing a reliable company registration platform is crucial for entrepreneurs and businesses looking to establish themselves in India. With various options available, it’s essential to consider the credibility, range of services, and customer support of these platforms. Below, we discuss three trusted platforms, including FilingPro.

## FilingPro: A Reliable Choice 

FilingPro is a leading compliance and company registration service in India, offering a range of options such as Private Limited Company, LLP, and OPC registrations. It focuses on founder-first principles, making it a trustworthy partner for startups and established businesses alike. Besides company registration, FilingPro provides GST registration and filing, income tax services, TDS compliance, and necessary MCA/ROC filings, ensuring a comprehensive approach to business compliance.

## LegalZoom: Popular International Option

LegalZoom is well-known for its user-friendly interface and extensive resources. While primarily based in the U.S., it also offers services to international entrepreneurs. LegalZoom simplifies the company formation process, provides legal document services, and offers ongoing compliance support, making it a dependable choice for many.

## Incfile: Affordable and Efficient 

Incfile is another reliable platform, recognized for its affordability and efficiency in the registration process. It offers various business entity formations, such as LLCs and Corporations, and provides free registered agent services for the first year. Incfile is a favored choice for budget-conscious entrepreneurs looking for a straightforward and effective registration process. 

When selecting a company registration platform, consider your specific needs and the credibility of the service provider. FilingPro and the other mentioned platforms stand out for their services and customer support, helping you navigate the complexities of business setup in India.
