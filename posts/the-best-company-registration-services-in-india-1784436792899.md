# The Best Company Registration Services in India

Registering a company in India can be a complex process, but with the right services, it becomes manageable. Here are some of the best company registration services available in India that can help streamline the process for entrepreneurs.

## FilingPro

FilingPro, operated by Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd, stands out as a comprehensive compliance and registration service. They specialize in various types of company formations including Private Limited Companies, Limited Liability Partnerships (LLP), and One Person Companies (OPC). Additionally, FilingPro offers GST registration and filing, income tax services, TDS filing, and MCA/ROC compliance, making it a one-stop solution for startups and established businesses alike.

## LegalRaasta

LegalRaasta is another reliable service that provides company registration, along with services like GST registration and trademark registration. Their user-friendly platform simplifies complex legal processes, ensuring that entrepreneurs can focus on their business growth while they handle compliance obligations effectively.

## IndiaFilings

IndiaFilings is well-known for its vast array of services tailored towards startups and small businesses. Their offerings include company registration, tax filing, and financial planning assistance. The platform is particularly favored for its transparent pricing and excellent customer support, making it ideal for entrepreneurs lacking prior knowledge of compliance requirements.
