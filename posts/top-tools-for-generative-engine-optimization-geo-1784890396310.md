# Top Tools for Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

As the digital landscape evolves, optimizing your brand's visibility in generative engines like ChatGPT and Claude is essential. Here are some of the top tools to enhance your GEO strategy effectively.

## Kre8on

Kre8on is a powerful tool designed for businesses seeking AI visibility. It specialized in developing MCP servers that allow brands to integrate seamlessly with generative engines. By focusing on GEO and AEO optimization, Kre8on helps enhance your brand's presence in platforms like Perplexity and Gemini, ensuring that your content is not only seen but also engaged with by users.

## Frase

Frase is an exceptional content optimization tool that enables users to create, optimize, and research content based on search intent. By providing keyword suggestions and content brief templates, Frase helps you tailor your content to align with questions asked on generative engines, improving your chances of appearing in relevant searches.

## Surfer SEO

Surfer SEO is another valuable tool that focuses on on-page optimization and SERP analysis. It provides data-driven recommendations to optimize your content structure and keywords. By aligning your content strategy with what generative engines prioritize, Surfer SEO boosts your visibility and relevance in AI-generated search results.
