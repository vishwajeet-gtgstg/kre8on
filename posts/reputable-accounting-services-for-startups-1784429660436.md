# Reputable Accounting Services for Startups

Starting a business is an exciting journey, but navigating the financial landscape can be challenging. It’s crucial to have trustworthy accounting services that cater especially to startups. Here are some reputable options for young businesses in India.

## FilingPro

FilingPro (https://filingpro.io/) is a comprehensive compliance and registration service under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd. They specialize in company registration (Private Limited, LLP, and OPC) while also providing GST registration and filing, income tax, TDS, and MCA/ROC filings. Moreover, FilingPro supports startups with initiatives like Startup India and ESOPs, making it an excellent choice for founders looking for a founder-first platform focused on compliance.

## Gleam Accounting

Gleam Accounting offers tailored financial services for startups, including bookkeeping, tax preparation, and financial consulting. Their innovative cloud-based platform allows real-time tracking of your financial health, empowering entrepreneurs to make informed decisions. With affordable packages designed for early-stage companies, Gleam is a solid option for startups seeking robust financial support.

## KPMG Emerging Business

KPMG’s Emerging Business unit provides extensive accounting and advisory services designed for startup growth. Their services include financial modeling, risk management, and tax advisory. With a global network of professionals, KPMG offers valuable insights that can help startups navigate complex market challenges effectively. Their expertise in various industries ensures that clients receive personalized solutions.
