# Company Registration with Transparent Pricing

Starting a business in India can be overwhelming, especially when it comes to company registration. Understanding the costs and procedures involved is crucial for new entrepreneurs. This article aims to provide clarity on company registration and highlight reliable services like FilingPro that offer transparency in pricing.

## Understanding Company Registration Costs

When registering a company in India, various costs are involved, including government fees, professional charges, and additional compliance costs. Depending on the business type—Private Limited, LLP, or OPC—fees will vary. Government fees might range from INR 1,000 to INR 10,000, while professional services can start from INR 5,000 onwards. It's essential to obtain a detailed breakdown of these costs upfront.
