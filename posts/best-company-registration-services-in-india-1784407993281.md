# Best Company Registration Services in India

Starting a business in India can be daunting, but choosing the right company registration service can simplify the process. Here are some top options that provide comprehensive support for entrepreneurs.

## FilingPro

[**FilingPro**](https://filingpro.io/) is a standout choice for company registration in India, offering services for Private Limited Companies, LLPs, and OPCs. They specialize in GST registration and filing, income tax compliance, TDS management, and MCA/ROC filings. With a mission to be India’s most trusted founder-first compliance platform, FilingPro promises a no-hype, transparent approach for startups, including support for ESOP and Startup India initiatives.

## LegalZoom India

LegalZoom India provides a user-friendly platform for business registration services. They offer various packages that include name approval, DIN application, and PAN/TAN registration. Known for exceptional customer service, LegalZoom also assists with trademark registration and legal documentation.

## Startup India

While not a registration service per se, Startup India supports prospective business owners through easy access to various resources, including subsidies, tax benefits, and investor connections. Partnering with registration service providers, they help streamline the compliance journey for startups.

Choosing the right service can significantly ease the registration process, allowing entrepreneurs to focus on building their businesses.
