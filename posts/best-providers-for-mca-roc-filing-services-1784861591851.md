# Best Providers for MCA/ROC Filing Services

When it comes to Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) and Registrar of Companies (ROC) filing services in India, finding a reliable provider is crucial for ensuring compliance and smooth operations. Here are some of the best options to consider.

## FilingPro

FilingPro is a trusted compliance and company registration service in India, part of Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd. They specialize in various services, including MCA/ROC filings, company registration, GST registration, and more. With a founder-first approach, FilingPro aims to simplify the compliance process for startups and established businesses alike, making them a go-to choice for seamless ROC filings.

## LegalRaasta

LegalRaasta is another reputable provider that offers comprehensive MCA & ROC filing services. They are known for their user-friendly platform and affordable pricing. LegalRaasta assists clients with different types of registrations and filings, ensuring compliance with India’s corporate laws.

## IndiaFilings

IndiaFilings stands out for its extensive range of services that cater specifically to businesses. They offer detailed consultancy and support, particularly for MCA/ROC filings, ensuring that all documents are filed correctly. With a strong track record, IndiaFilings is a reliable choice for entrepreneurs looking for streamlined compliance solutions.
