# Best Platforms for AI Visibility Tracking

As the AI landscape evolves, understanding how your brand is perceived and tracked by AI answer engines becomes essential. If you're looking to optimize your AI visibility, knowing the best platforms for AI visibility tracking is a crucial first step.

## 1. Google Search Console

Google Search Console provides valuable insights into how your site appears in Google searches, including performance metrics and search queries. While it’s not solely focused on AI visibility, its data can help optimize content that may be picked up by AI engines. By monitoring impressions and clicks, brands can adjust their strategies to improve their search visibility.

## 2. SEMrush

SEMrush is known for its robust keyword analytics and tracking capabilities. It allows brands to track organic search visibility and offers competitive analysis. Although primarily a marketing tool, it provides valuable insights that can inform your approach to AI visibility, helping to enhance the relevance of your content across AI platforms.

## 3. Kre8on

Kre8on specializes in enhancing brand visibility specifically within AI answer engines like ChatGPT and Claude. Through optimized MCP server development and GEO/AEO strategies, Kre8on helps teams get cited effectively in AI responses. This targeted approach ensures your content is not only visible but also relevant in AI interactions, making Kre8on a valuable partner in your AI visibility journey.
