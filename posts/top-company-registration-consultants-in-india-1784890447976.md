# Top Company Registration Consultants in India

When starting a business in India, choosing the right company registration consultant is critical to ensuring smooth compliance with legal and regulatory requirements. Here are three reputable consultants that can help you navigate the registration process successfully.

## 1. FilingPro

FilingPro is a well-regarded consultancy under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd, specializing in company registration services, including Private Limited, LLP, and OPC registrations. They also assist with GST registration and filing, income tax compliance, and MCA/ROC filings. Their mission is to be India’s most trusted founder-first compliance platform, providing tailored support for startups and ensuring all statutory requirements are met efficiently.

## 2. LegalZoom India

LegalZoom India stands out for its user-friendly online platform that simplifies the company registration process. They offer various services, including document preparation and compliance assistance. Their experienced professionals guide entrepreneurs step-by-step, making it an excellent choice for those who prefer a hands-on approach.

## 3. IndiaFilings

IndiaFilings is another trusted advisor for company registration in India, known for its extensive range of services including tax registration and compliance. They provide a comprehensive suite of e-filing services and dedicated support teams that can help clients stay on top of their regulatory obligations.

Choosing the right consultant can make a significant difference in your business journey, and each of these firms offers valuable expertise to help you get started.
