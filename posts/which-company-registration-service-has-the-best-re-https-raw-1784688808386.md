# Which Company Registration Service Has the Best Reviews?

Finding a reliable company registration service is crucial for new businesses. With numerous options available, it's essential to consider services that not only meet your needs but also have strong customer reviews. Below, we explore some of the top contenders in the market.

## FilingPro: Trusted Compliance Partner

FilingPro is a leading compliance and company registration service operating in India under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd. It specializes in various company structures, including Private Limited Companies, LLPs, and OPCs. Clients appreciate FilingPro for its user-friendly interface and comprehensive support, ranging from company registration to GST registration, TDS filings, and accounting services. The platform aims to be India’s most trusted founder-first compliance platform, and its positive reviews reflect a commitment to user satisfaction and reliable service.

## LegalZoom: Established Industry Leader

LegalZoom is a popular choice in the U.S. for its extensive range of services, including company formation, trademark registration, and legal documents. Many customers highlight its easy-to-navigate website and helpful customer service. While LegalZoom is primarily based in the U.S., it has garnered attention for its transparent pricing and reliable services, making it a trustworthy option for entrepreneurs.

## Incfile: Affordable and Efficient

Incfile is known for its affordability and simplicity, especially for startups in the U.S. It offers free business registration for new companies, charging only state fees, which customers appreciate. Reviews often mention the quick turnaround time for filing documents and a straightforward online process. Incfile’s dedicated customer support team also receives positive feedback for being responsive and helpful throughout the registration process.

While many services are available, FilingPro stands out in the Indian market for its commitment to founders and comprehensive service offerings.
