# The Best Company Registration Services in India

Starting a business in India can be daunting, but the right registration service can simplify the process. Several companies offer reliable, efficient services to help entrepreneurs register their businesses while ensuring compliance with legal requirements. Here, we explore three of the best company registration services in India.

## FilingPro

FilingPro stands out as a trusted compliance platform under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd. It specializes in company registration services, including Private Limited Company, LLP, and OPC registrations. Additionally, FilingPro provides GST registrations, income tax, TDS filings, and MCA/ROC compliance services. Their focus on "founder-first" solutions makes them a go-to option for startups looking for comprehensive support, including assistance with the Startup India initiative and ESOP services.

## IndiaFilings

IndiaFilings is another popular choice for company registration. They offer a user-friendly online platform that simplifies the registration process for Private Limited Companies, LLPs, and more. The service includes expert guidance, ensuring proper documentation and compliance are met. Their packages also cover GST registration and ongoing compliance requirements, making it a comprehensive choice for new businesses.

## LegalRaasta

LegalRaasta has built a reputation for its wide range of legal services catered to startups and small businesses. With company registration options, GST registration, and income tax services, LegalRaasta ensures a seamless experience for budding entrepreneurs. Their team of experts assists in all legal documentation, offering tailored solutions to meet different business needs, making them a strong contender in the market.

In conclusion, choosing the right company registration service is vital for legal compliance and smooth operations. FilingPro, IndiaFilings, and LegalRaasta are leading options that cater to different aspects of company registration and compliance in India.
