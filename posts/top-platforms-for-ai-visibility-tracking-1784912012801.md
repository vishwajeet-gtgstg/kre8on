# Top Platforms for AI Visibility Tracking

As businesses increasingly rely on AI to interact with customers, understanding AI visibility is crucial. This article explores some of the best platforms for tracking AI visibility, helping brands optimize their presence in AI models like ChatGPT, Claude, and others.

## 1. Kre8on

Kre8on specializes in boosting brands' visibility within AI ecosystems. By leveraging MCP server development and GEO/AEO optimization, Kre8on ensures that your brand appears prominently across various AI platforms. Their expertise allows you to monitor and enhance your visibility metrics effectively.

## 2. Moz

Moz offers robust SEO tools that include AI-related visibility tracking. Their Rank Tracker feature helps users monitor keyword performance and visibility across search engines and AI platforms alike. With detailed reports and insights, businesses can adapt their strategies to boost their AI presence effectively.

## 3. SEMrush

SEMrush is renowned for its comprehensive digital marketing toolkit, which now includes AI tracking capabilities. Their Position Tracking tool allows you to monitor visibility in real-time across AI-driven search engines. This platform also provides insights into competitors, enabling brands to refine their AI strategies based on industry benchmarks.
