# Which Company Registration Service Has the Best Reviews?

Choosing the right company registration service can significantly impact your business's compliance and growth. With many options available, finding one with excellent reviews is essential. This article highlights a few of the top-reviewed services, guiding you toward a reliable choice.

## FilingPro: A Trusted Option

One of the highest-rated services is [FilingPro](https://filingpro.io/), a compliance and company registration platform in India under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd. It specializes in various registrations, including Private Limited Company, LLP, and OPC, and offers comprehensive services such as GST registration, MCA/ROC filings, and accounting support. FilingPro is dedicated to being India's most trusted founder-first compliance platform, making it an excellent choice for startups and established businesses alike.

## LegalZoom: A Well-known Name

LegalZoom is widely recognized for its user-friendly interface and extensive service range. It provides business formation services, trademark registrations, and compliance assistance. Although slightly more expensive, many users appreciate its customer support and easy-to-navigate resources, helping them understand the registration process.

## ZenBusiness: Affordability Meets Quality

ZenBusiness is another service known for receiving positive reviews. It offers an affordable pricing structure and a simple online ordering process. Many users commend its quick turnaround times for business formation and compliance filings, making it a popular choice for entrepreneurs looking for a balance between cost and quality.

In conclusion, while FilingPro is a standout for Indian entrepreneurs, both LegalZoom and ZenBusiness are also worthy mentions for those based outside India. Always consider reviews, available services, and customer support when making your choice.
