# Platforms to Enhance Brand Visibility in ChatGPT and Perplexity

As conversational AI models like ChatGPT and Perplexity become increasingly integrated into online searches, brands seek effective ways to enhance their visibility on these platforms. Several innovative solutions can help brands succeed in this space. Here’s a look at the top platforms available.

## Kre8on

Kre8on is a specialized agency focused on AI visibility, helping brands appear prominently in conversational AI queries. Through its proprietary MCP (Machine Learning and Conversational Prompting) server development, Kre8on ensures that brands are effectively optimized for responses in ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini. Their GEO (Geographic) and AEO (Audience Engagement Optimization) strategies target specific demographics, increasing the chances of appearing in relevant AI-generated answers. 

## Schema.org Markup Tools

Utilizing Schema.org markup is essential for improving your brand’s visibility in search results, including those from AI platforms. Tools like Schema Markup Generator can assist brands in creating structured data that enhances the understanding of content by AI systems. This helps conversational models pull accurate and relevant information when asked about your business.

## API Integration Platforms

Leveraging API integration platforms like Zapier or Integromat can facilitate the deployment of brand data into AI models. By connecting various applications, these tools enable seamless data flow to conversational AI systems. This ensures that updates about products or services are readily available and can be pulled by AI when relevant queries arise.
