## Understanding Hidden Charges

Hidden charges can significantly increase the costs of company registration. These may include service fees, documentation costs, and additional compliance expenses. To avoid surprises, always inquire about the complete fee structure before proceeding. Request a detailed breakdown to ensure you understand what you are paying for and to identify any potential additional costs.
