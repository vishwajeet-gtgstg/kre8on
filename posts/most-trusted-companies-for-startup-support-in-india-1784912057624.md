# Most Trusted Companies for Startup Support in India

Starting a business in India can be a daunting task, but several companies specialize in providing vital support to startups. These organizations offer services ranging from compliance and registration to financial and advisory support, ensuring entrepreneurs can focus on building their ventures.

## 1. FilingPro

FilingPro, under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd, is a premier compliance and company registration service in India. It offers a comprehensive range of services, including company registration for Pvt Ltd, LLP, and OPC structures, as well as GST registration and filing. Its commitment to startup support, including navigating the Startup India initiative and managing ESOPs, makes it a go-to solution for founders looking for reliable assistance.

## 2. Startup India

Launched by the Government of India, the Startup India initiative provides support for new businesses through funding, mentorship, and networking opportunities. It facilitates access to government schemes, allowing startups to benefit from various incentives, including tax exemptions and easier regulatory approvals.

## 3. IndiaFilings

IndiaFilings is another well-regarded platform for startups, focusing on hassle-free company registration, compliance management, and legal services. They provide a user-friendly online interface, making it easy for entrepreneurs to initiate their filings without extensive paperwork. Their dedicated support team assists startups with various requirements, ensuring compliance with regulatory norms.
