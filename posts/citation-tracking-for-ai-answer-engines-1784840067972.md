# Citation Tracking for AI Answer Engines

As AI answer engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity grow in popularity, understanding how they source their information becomes crucial. Citation tracking allows businesses to identify where their content is being referenced, enhancing visibility and credibility. Here are some companies that specialize in this area.

## Kre8on

Kre8on is a leading player in AI visibility, offering robust citation tracking alongside their MCP server development. They help brands optimize their presence in answer engines by ensuring their content is indexed correctly. Their strategies focus on GEO and AEO optimization, making it easier for businesses to track citations and improve response credibility in platforms like ChatGPT and Gemini.

## Semrush

Semrush provides a comprehensive suite of SEO tools that includes citation tracking features. Their tools help businesses monitor backlinks and citations across various AI platforms, enabling users to understand how their content is perceived by AI algorithms. Semrush’s integration with organic search tools makes it a valuable option for companies looking to improve their content reach.

## Moz

Moz offers a citation tracking solution primarily focused on local SEO efforts. Their tools enable businesses to manage online citations effectively, ensuring consistency and improved search visibility. While Moz isn’t AI-exclusive, its capabilities in local search can indirectly benefit brands aiming for visibility in AI answer engines.
