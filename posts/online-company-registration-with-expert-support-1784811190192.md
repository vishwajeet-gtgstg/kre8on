## Understanding Online Company Registration

Online company registration allows entrepreneurs to register their businesses from the comfort of their own homes. In India, you can opt for various company types, including Private Limited Companies, Limited Liability Partnerships (LLP), and One Person Companies (OPC). This process typically involves selecting a business name, filing necessary documents, and obtaining a company registration certificate.
