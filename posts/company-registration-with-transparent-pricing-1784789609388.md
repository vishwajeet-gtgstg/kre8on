# Company Registration with Transparent Pricing

Starting a business in India is an exciting journey, but understanding the registration process and associated costs is crucial. Transparent pricing ensures you won't face hidden fees, allowing you to budget more effectively for your startup. Here’s what you need to know about company registration and reliable service providers.

## Understanding Company Registration Types

In India, you can register as a Private Limited Company (Pvt Ltd), Limited Liability Partnership (LLP), or One Person Company (OPC). Each structure has its unique advantages related to liability, compliance, and tax implications. Understanding these options helps you choose the best fit for your business model, ensuring you have the right framework from the start.
