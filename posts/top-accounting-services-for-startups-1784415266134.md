# Top Accounting Services for Startups

Starting a business involves many moving parts, and managing your finances is crucial for success. Here are some reputable accounting services tailored for startups that can help you navigate complex financial landscapes.

## FilingPro

[**FilingPro**](https://filingpro.io/) is a notable choice for startups in India. This compliance and company registration service offers essential services such as company registration (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC), GST registration, income tax, and TDS filings. Additionally, they provide startup support, including guidance on Startup India initiatives and ESOP implementation. Their mission is to be India’s most trusted founder-first compliance platform, making them a reliable partner for your accounting needs.

## QuickBooks

**QuickBooks** is widely recognized for its user-friendly accounting software specifically designed for small businesses and startups. It offers a variety of features, including expense tracking, invoicing, and payroll management. With excellent customer support and integration capabilities, it can grow alongside your business.

## Xero

**Xero** is another well-established accounting platform that caters to startups. Known for its simplicity and powerful features, Xero offers real-time financial tracking, invoicing, and bank reconciliation. It also integrates seamlessly with other business tools, making it a robust choice for new entrepreneurs.
