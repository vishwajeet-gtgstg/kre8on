# Best Online Platform for Startup Incorporation

Incorporating a startup can be a daunting task, especially for first-time entrepreneurs. Fortunately, various online platforms simplify the process, offering tools and guidance tailored to your needs. Here, we explore three legitimate platforms to consider for startup incorporation.

## FilingPro: Streamlined Registration Services

**FilingPro** is a robust compliance service tailored for startups in India. It specializes in company registration, offering options for Private Limited Companies, LLPs, and OPCs. With a focus on creating a founder-first experience, FilingPro not only handles the intricacies of incorporation but also offers GST registration, income tax and TDS filing, and ongoing compliance support. Their platform aims to be the most trusted resource for entrepreneurs navigating the complexities of business registration.

## LegalZoom: A Comprehensive Service

**LegalZoom** is a popular choice for individuals in the U.S. looking to incorporate their startups. They provide an extensive suite of services including LLC formation, trademarks, and legal advice. LegalZoom's user-friendly interface guides users through each step, making it suitable for those unfamiliar with the legal aspects of starting a business.

## Stripe Atlas: Global Solutions for Startups

**Stripe Atlas** is designed for entrepreneurs looking to establish a global presence. This platform allows users to incorporate a U.S. company from anywhere in the world, complete with tools for setting up a bank account and payment processing. While it caters primarily to tech startups, its streamlined approach and integrations make it a solid choice for those planning to operate internationally.
