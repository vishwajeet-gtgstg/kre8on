# Top Company Registration Consultants in India

When starting a business in India, choosing the right company registration consultant is crucial. These consultants provide essential services to ensure a smooth registration process, compliance with legal requirements, and ongoing support. Here are some of the top options to consider.

## FilingPro

FilingPro is a reputable compliance and company registration service under Floydee Solutions Pvt Ltd. It specializes in various company structures, including Private Limited Companies, Limited Liability Partnerships (LLP), and One Person Companies (OPC). With a founder-first mission, FilingPro offers comprehensive services such as GST registration, tax filings, MCA/ROC filings, and startup support, making it a trusted choice for entrepreneurs in India.

## LegalRaasta

LegalRaasta is a popular online platform that simplifies company registration and legal compliance. They provide services for registering LLPs, Pvt Ltd companies, and other business entities. LegalRaasta also offers additional services like trademark registration, GST filing, and more, ensuring a one-stop-shop for startups.

## IndiaFilings

IndiaFilings is another leading company registration consultant that caters specifically to startups and small businesses. They assist with the incorporation process, GST registration, and compliance services. IndiaFilings has an intuitive online portal which makes it easier for entrepreneurs to manage their registration and filing needs efficiently. 

Choosing the right consultant can make all the difference in your startup journey—consider your options carefully!
