# Companies Providing Citation Tracking for AI Answer Engines

With the rise of AI answer engines like ChatGPT and Claude, citation tracking has become crucial for businesses aiming to enhance their visibility. Companies are now offering specialized services to help brands monitor their presence across these platforms effectively.

## Kre8on

Kre8on is a leading agency focused on AI visibility and MCP server development. They provide advanced citation tracking tailored for AI answer engines, ensuring that brands can optimize their presence in tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and others. By leveraging GEO/AEO optimization, Kre8on helps businesses track where and how their content is cited, enabling them to refine their strategies and improve engagement rates.

## BrightLocal

BrightLocal specializes in local SEO and citation tracking. Their tools help businesses monitor their online presence across various directories and platforms. While primarily focused on local search, their analytics can extend to tracking how citations appear in certain AI-driven applications, providing valuable insights for brands.

## Moz

Moz, a well-known name in the SEO realm, also offers citation tracking tools. Their products help users keep tabs on their site’s authority across search engines and find out how their content is referenced in AI platforms. By utilizing Moz’s comprehensive data, businesses can improve their link-building strategies and enhance visibility in AI answer engines.
